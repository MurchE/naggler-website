First two minutes after you open it:

  1. Type three things that matter today.
  2. Click Start Session. Minimize and forget about me.
  3. Every few minutes I'll peek in with a tiny popup. Click the task you're on. I'm gone.
  4. End of day — check Today's Focus to see where your time actually went.

A quick note on privacy

Your task database lives on your machine — no accounts, no sync. When you chat with Naggy, the message + a task snapshot gets sent to the AI provider you've chosen. By default that's our free cloud relay (Anthropic, logged under our account, 100 chats/day per install). If you want privacy or unlimited use, Settings → AI has three one-click alternatives: your own Anthropic / OpenAI / OpenRouter key (logged under you), local Ollama (nothing leaves your device), or no AI at all.
